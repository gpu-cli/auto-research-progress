# Design

## Direction
An engineering journal with the pacing of a high-end independent publication: strong editorial type, evidence-sized media, technical labels, and hairline rules. Layout—not decoration—creates hierarchy.

## Typography
- Display: Newsreader Variable, 420–500 weight, tight optical display tracking.
- Interface: Manrope Variable, compact and direct.
- Metadata/code: JetBrains Mono Variable.
- Rendered Markdown: shadcn/typeset with Newsreader body, Manrope headings, and an 18px / 1.78 reading rhythm.

## Color
Warm paper (`#f2f0e9`), green-black ink (`#191d1b`), vermilion action/evidence accent (`#e34a29`), restrained green for verified/published state. Dark mode keeps the same tinted character.

## Composition
- Homepage: large journal masthead, one lead investigation, then ruled archive rows.
- Entry: editorial title and deck, full-width evidence, then a narrow 760px reading measure.
- Never place experiment imagery in tiny decorative thumbnails when it is the evidence.

## Motion
Only subtle image scale and state transitions. Respect reduced motion.

## Accessibility
Visible focus, semantic hierarchy, 44px-equivalent mobile controls, readable contrast, native video controls, captions/alt text supported in the media schema.
