# Product

## Audience
Forge engineers and research collaborators deciding which paper ideas, optimizations, and runtime changes deserve implementation or promotion.

## Job to be done
Show what changed, what evidence exists, what remains uncertain, and where the underlying artifacts can be inspected. The archive is permanent and newest-first.

## Voice
Editorial, technical, precise, candid. Conclusions lead; caveats remain visible. Never invent metrics or ornamental dashboard data.

## Surface
Primary: Monitor. Secondary: Learn / Inspect on entry pages. This is not a marketing landing page.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards, equal-weight feature cards, indigo gradients, glass surfaces, icon-topped tiles, fake metrics, and nested cards.
